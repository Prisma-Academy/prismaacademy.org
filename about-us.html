About us

Prisma Academy, a not-for-profit organization, helps young people and graduates build strong networks and transition smoothly into their careers. Formerly Infinite Development, it now focuses on mentorship, skill workshops for professional growth, and unity gatherings for social integration to support career and personal success.

Working Together to Succeed

Prisma Academy serves a diverse community, starting with assisting individuals in choosing their field of study at university and college, and transitioning to professional work in their area. We have hosted more than 50 programs, aiming to help participants grow, build confidence, and develop social and professional skills for their future careers.

Together we can make a huge impact.
